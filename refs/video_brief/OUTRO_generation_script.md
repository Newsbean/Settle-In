# SETTLE IN — Outro Video Generation Script (~15s)

Purpose: the closing "welcome home" payoff. Mirror of the intro — the same
rooms, now fully lived-in and warm. The genre's beloved before/after reward as
a short, tender victory lap. Wordless until the final card.

Reference frames (feed as image prompt / style anchor):
- `reference_style_warm-room.jpg` — the warm room, coral gouache walls, wood
  floor, crooked picture, ceiling glow. Look-lock for the whole outro; imagine
  each room now furnished with the assembled flat-pack pieces.
- `reference_arrival_doorway.jpg` — the doorway; reuse for the closing pull-back
  so intro and outro rhyme.

---

## GLOBAL STYLE BLOCK (paste into every shot / the style field)

> First-person point of view, handheld-calm. Storybook **gouache** render:
> flat matte surfaces, zero specular, no gloss, no PBR realism. Soft, toy-like,
> hand-painted picture-book world. Fully warm golden hour — every room lit warm
> `#ffd9a0`, lighting is the reward. Muted domestic palette — coral-rose walls
> `#e7a9a0`/`#d98b82`, cream ceiling, pale wood-plank floor `#e2c69f`, warm wood
> furniture `#cf9a6f`/`#a9744c`, coral `#e88b7d`, gold `#f0c55f`, sky `#8bb8c7`,
> lilac `#b98bbf`, amber `#e5a85a`. Never photoreal, never saturated primaries,
> never pure white. Contented, tender, complete. 66° FOV, no head-bob, slow
> eased moves.

**Negative prompt:** photorealistic, PBR, glossy, reflective, harsh shadows,
lens flare, neon, saturated primaries, text overlay, UI, HUD, watermark, people,
faces, fast cuts, camera shake, motion blur, fisheye distortion, mess, clutter,
empty rooms.

**Audio direction (if the tool scores it):** warm room tone; a soft rising
"task complete" arpeggio chime once early; a final warm celesta chord on the
candle beat. Quiet, rooted in daily life.

---

## SHOT LIST (times sum to ~15s)

**Shot 1 — 0:00–0:06 · The finished home**
POV slow pan across a now-furnished, fully warm living room: an assembled wooden
bookshelf with a neat row of books stepping evenly in height, a soft two-tone
sofa with plumped cushions, a low table on a rug, the once-crooked picture now
perfectly level. In the kitchen corner beyond: a mug, plant and kettle on the
counter and a small shelf of spice jars. Golden light, everything in its place.

**Shot 2 — 0:06–0:11 · The pull-back**
Camera slowly retreats toward the front door we entered through in the intro,
the warm furnished rooms receding behind it — the before/after rhyme made
physical. A few warm sparkles (`#fff2cf`) drift down as the light reaches its
fullest swell.

**Shot 3 — 0:11–0:15 · Welcome home**
Hold on the glowing home from the threshold. A soft candle flame flickers into
view. Hand-lettered text fades up centered: **welcome home** 🕯 (lowercase,
gentle). A final warm celesta chord. Slow fade to warm cream.

## ONE-PARAGRAPH VERSION (for single-prompt tools like Kling / Sora / Veo — 15s)

> First-person POV, storybook gouache style, flat matte no-gloss hand-painted
> world, warm and toy-like, full golden-hour glow. Slowly pan across a fully
> furnished, cozy living room — assembled wooden bookshelf with books stepping
> evenly by height, a soft sofa with plumped cushions, a low table on a rug, a
> perfectly level framed picture of green hills, a tidy kitchen nook with a mug,
> plant and kettle beyond. The camera then slowly pulls back toward the front
> door, the warm rooms receding behind it as gentle sparkles drift and the light
> swells to its fullest, ending on the glowing home with a soft candle flame.
> Muted domestic palette, no pure white, no saturated colors, 66° FOV, no
> head-bob, slow eased moves, contented and tender. Negative: photoreal, glossy,
> harsh shadows, text mid-scene, UI, people, fast cuts, camera shake, clutter,
> empty rooms.
