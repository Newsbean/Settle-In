# SETTLE IN — Intro Video Generation Script (~15s)

Purpose: the opening title beat. Arriving at a home that's been empty for
years and quietly beginning to re-inhabit it. Wordless, warm, patient. No
gameplay HUD, no logos until the end card.

Reference frames (feed as image prompt / style anchor):
- `reference_arrival_doorway.jpg` — POV at the front door, a taped parcel and the
  order-laptop just inside. Use for shot 1.
- `reference_style_warm-room.jpg` — the warm empty living room, coral gouache
  walls, pale wood floor, a crooked framed picture, a soft ceiling glow. Use for
  shots 2–3 and as the overall look-lock.

---

## GLOBAL STYLE BLOCK (paste into every shot / the style field)

> First-person point of view, handheld-calm. Storybook **gouache** render:
> flat matte surfaces, zero specular, no gloss, no PBR realism. Soft, toy-like,
> hand-painted picture-book world. Warm afternoon sun through curtains.
> Muted domestic palette — coral-rose walls `#e7a9a0`/`#d98b82`, cream ceiling,
> pale wood-plank floor `#e2c69f`, warm wood `#cf9a6f`, cardboard `#d8a86f`,
> gentle accents coral `#e88b7d`, gold `#f0c55f`, sky `#8bb8c7`, lilac `#b98bbf`.
> Never photoreal, never saturated primaries, never pure white. Quiet, patient,
> tender mood. 66° comfortable FOV, no head-bob, slow eased camera moves.

**Negative prompt:** photorealistic, PBR, glossy, reflective, harsh shadows,
lens flare, neon, saturated primaries, text overlay, UI, HUD, watermark, people,
faces, fast cuts, camera shake, motion blur, fisheye distortion, clutter.

**Audio direction (if the tool scores it):** near-silence with room tone; faint
birdsong outside; a single gentle piano/celesta note when the light swells.
Everything quiet and rooted in daily life.

---

## SHOT LIST (times sum to ~15s)

**Shot 1 — 0:00–0:05 · Crossing the threshold**
Slow forward dolly through a wood-trimmed doorway in a coral-rose exterior wall,
into a dim, empty living room. A taped cardboard parcel (shipping-label doodle,
up-arrows) rests just inside; a small laptop sits open on a wooden crate by the
door. The interior is a touch cool and underlit — a home that's been waiting.

**Shot 2 — 0:05–0:10 · The first warmth**
The camera settles in the empty room: coral gouache walls, pale wood floor, a
single crooked framed picture of green hills on the wall. A warm ceiling light
**slowly swells** from dim to a soft golden glow (`#ffd9a0`), washing the walls
warmer. Tiny warm sparkles (`#fff2cf`) drift briefly. The house waking up.

**Shot 3 — 0:10–0:15 · Title**
Hold on the now-warm room as the crooked picture is gently, magically nudged
level. Hand-lettered title fades up centered: **SETTLE IN** — smaller line
beneath: *make it feel like home again.* One warm celesta note. Fade to warm
cream.
